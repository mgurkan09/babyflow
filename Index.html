# Privacy Policy for BabyFlow **Last Updated:** January 1, 2026 ## Introduction BabyFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. ## Information We Collect ### Personal Information - **Baby Profile Data**: Name, birth date, gender, weight, height - **Health Records**: Feeding times, sleep patterns, diaper changes, growth measurements, vaccination records, health notes - **Photos and Videos**: Baby memories and milestone photos stored locally on your device ### Automatically Collected Information - **Device Information**: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers - **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration, crash reports - **Advertising ID**: Google Advertising ID for personalized ads (optional, can be opted out) ## How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: - Provide and maintain the app functionality - Track your baby's growth and development - Send reminder notifications for feedings, sleep, and other activities - Improve app performance and user experience - Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob - Analyze app usage and fix technical issues ## Data Storage and Security ### Local Storage - **All personal data** (baby profiles, health records, photos) is stored **locally on your device only** - We do NOT store your personal information on external servers - Data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us ### Security Measures - Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest - We use industry-standard security protocols - Sensitive authentication data is excluded from backups - HTTPS-only communication for network requests ### Backups - Android Backup Service may backup your data to your Google Account - You can disable backups in your device settings - Sensitive authentication files are excluded from backups ## Data Sharing and Disclosure We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. ### Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services: 1. **Google AdMob** (Advertising) - Purpose: Display advertisements - Data Shared: Advertising ID, device information, app usage data - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy - You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings 2. **Google Firebase** (Analytics & Authentication - if enabled) - Purpose: App analytics, crash reporting, user authentication - Data Shared: Usage statistics, crash logs, authentication tokens - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy 3. **Google Play Services** - Purpose: In-app purchases, location services, billing - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy ### Legal Requirements We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority. ## Children's Privacy BabyFlow is designed for use by parents and caregivers (adults 18+), NOT by children under 13. ### COPPA Compliance - We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 - The app collects information ABOUT children (entered by parents) but not FROM children - Parents have full control over all data entered into the app - Parents can delete all data at any time If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. ## Your Data Rights You have the right to: ### Access and Control - **View**: Access all data stored in the app - **Edit**: Modify any information about your baby - **Delete**: Remove individual records or all data from the app - **Export**: Export your data to PDF or Excel format ### Account Deletion - To delete your account and all associated data, uninstall the app from your device - All locally stored data will be permanently removed - If you used cloud backup, delete the backup from your Google Account settings ### Opt-Out Rights - **Notifications**: Disable in app settings or device notification settings - **Personalized Ads**: Opt-out in device settings > Google > Ads - **Analytics**: Disable in app settings (if available) ## Data Retention - Data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app - We do not retain data on our servers (as we don't collect it) - Backup data retention follows your Google Account backup settings ## International Data Transfers Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers by us. However, third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase) may transfer data internationally according to their privacy policies. ## Medical Disclaimer **IMPORTANT**: BabyFlow is NOT a medical device and should NOT replace professional medical advice. - The app is for tracking and informational purposes only - Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions - In emergencies, contact your doctor or emergency services immediately - We are not liable for any health decisions made based on app data ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
